* Comparison Analysis -> It invovles comparing different variable, groups , or scenario to identify similarities, difference,pattern,trends

ex- > compare sales and profit across the region

* Ranking Analysis -> Process of evaluting and analying the ranking of items, entities or data based on
certain criteria or attributes.

Ex- > find top 5 product in item of sales


* Contribution Analysis-> It is powerful tool that helps the business and organization to
Analyze the contribution of various factor towards the particular outcome

Ex- > Analyze the contribution of category towards the sales , revenue etc.


* Pareto Analysis-> It is also known as 80/20 rule , is a management tool used to identify the 
most significant factor or causes that contribute to particular outcome. in pareto analysis it is assumed that
80 % of the effects are caused by 20% of the factors.
